TS EAMCET(scholarship)

Students Eligible :

  • Candidates must obtain at least 25% of the maximum marks in the TS EAMCET exam.
  • SC & ST Welfare Students whose annual Family income is Rs. Two Lakhs or below.
  • BC & EBC and Minority Welfare Students who belongs to rural area their family income should be Rs. One Lakh Fifty Thousand or below.
  • BC & EBC and Minority Welfare Students who are belongs to Urban area family income should be Rs. Two Lakhs or below.
  • Disabled Welfare Students whose parental income is Rs. One lakh or below.
  • EBC students who are selected Under Corporate College Admissions Scheme Eligible for Intermediate courses.
  • Students whose attendance is 75% at the end of each quarter & Promoted to next academic year for Renewal Students.

Students not Eligible :

  • Students belonging to the categories other than SC, ST, BC, EBC , Minority and DW(Disabled).
  • SC,ST Students whose annual family income is more than Rs. Two Lakhs.
  • BC & EBC and Minority Rural area Students Whose annual family income is more than Rs. One Lakh Fifty Thousand.
  • BC & EBC and Minority Urban area Students Whose annual family income is more than Rs. Two Lakhs.
  • Disabled Welfare Students whose family income is more than Rupees One lakh.
  • All Students who are pursuing the part time courses, online courses & Correspondence courses.
  • Students admitted under Sponsored seats, Management Quota seats & Spot Admissions.
  • Students who applied Stipend/Scholorship in any Scheme.
  • Students of BC, EBC and DW students studying the Courses offered by open universities, distant mode, category B seats in MBBS, BDS.
  • EBC students studying Intermediate or courses.

TSWREIS(Free Education Test)

Students Eligible :

  • students belonging to Scheduled Castes (SC), Scheduled Tribes (ST), and Other Backward Classes (OBC) communities in Telangana.
  • Reserved category students should be between 9 to 13 years and for others the age limit is between 9 to 11.
  • Should have studied in the same district for two consecutive years i.e class 3rd and class 4th.
  • The candidates should have studied continuously in the respective district for the two academic years.
  • Education must be from the Government school or in a school recognized by the Government of Telangana.
  • The annual income of the parents should not be more than Rs.1,50,000/- in Rural areas and Rs.2.00,000/- in Urban areas.

National Overseas Scholarship
The National Overseas Scholarship was launched by the Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment. Through this scheme, the Government provides scholarships to the students who want to study overseas. This scholarship is helpful for those students who come from a low financial background.

Eligibility Criteria of National Overseas Scholarship

  • The candidate must be a permanent resident of India.
  • The maximum age to get eligible for this scholarship is 35 years.
  • The candidate must have at least 60% in qualifying exams.
  • The candidate’s family income must not be more than Rs 8 lakh.
  • Only two children from the same family are eligible for this scholarship.
  • The scholarship cannot be awarded more than one time.

Types of Assistance in Value of National Overseas Scholarship

  • Annual maintenance allowance
  • Tuition fees
  • Medical insurance premium
  • Poll tax
  • Contingency allowance
  • Visa fees
  • Incidental journey allowance and equipment allowance

The JN Tata Endowment Loan Scholarship
The JN Tata endowment loan scholarship is dedicated to Indians who want to study overseas and pursue higher education. This scholarship supports the students at the postgraduate/doctoral/postdoctoral and research level. The JN Tata endowment loan scholarship has a simple interest of 2% per annum. This scholarship was initiated in 1892 and has offered financial support to over 5,350 students.

Eligibility Criteria of The JN Tata Endowment Loan Scholarship

  • The candidates must be permanent residents of India.
  • The maximum eligibility age is 45 years (30 June 2022).
  • The candidates must have completed one undergraduate degree.
  • The candidate must have at least 60% in qualifying exams.
  • The admitted candidate must be recognized as a graduate program.

Types of Assistance in Value of National Overseas Scholarship

  • The students who are selected for The JN Tata endowment loan scholarship will get a loan scholarship up to Rs 10 Lakh. They also get travel entitled for a “Travel grant” and a “Gift award,” while the travel grants amount RS 50,000 to RS 7,50,000.

3. Fulbright-Nehru Master’s Fellowships
The Fulbright-Nehru Master’s Fellowships are made for Indian candidates who are talented in their studies, have good work experience, and have decent education qualifications.

Eligibility Criteria of Fulbright-Nehru Master’s Fellowships

  • The candidate must have four years of bachelor’s degree or completed a master’s degree or full-time postgraduate diploma.
  • The candidate must have a minimum of 55% or higher in an undergraduate degree.
  • Must have good leadership skills and community service experience.
  • The minimum IELTS score must be 6.0.

Types of Assistance in Value of Fulbright-Nehru Master’s Fellowships

  • Scholarships provide J-1 visa support.
  • Round trip of economic class in air travel from home country to the USA.
  • Funding for tuition.
  • Coverage of accidents and sickness as per US government guidelines.
View More ▼
4. Fulbright-Nehru Master’s Doctoral Research Fellowships
The Fulbright-Nehru Master’s Doctoral Research Fellowships are designed for Indian students who are registered for a Ph.D. at an Indian Institute. These fellowships are for 6-9 months. Under these fellowships, Indian students are invited from large fields such as Computer science, public health, chemistry, bioengineering, and more.

Eligibility Criteria of Fulbright-Nehru Master’s Doctoral Research Fellowships

  • The applicant must be registered for Ph.D. at an Indian Institute.
  • The candidate should have a high level of academic and professional achievement. The candidate should have conducted research in a relevant field.
  • Employed applicants should have the employer’s endorsement.

Types of Assistance in Value of Fulbright-Nehru Master’s Doctoral Research Fellowships

  • J – 1 visa support
  • A monthly stipend
  • Coverage of accidents and sickness as per US government guidelines.
  • Round trip of economic class in air travel
  • No fees and applicable allowances on dependents.

5. Great Wall Program
The great wall program is a scholarship which is established by the Chinese Government, and UNESCO is sponsoring the students and scholars in developing countries to study and conduct research in China. This program supports general and senior scholars.

Eligibility Criteria of Great Wall Program

  • The applicant must not be a citizen of the People’s Republic of China.
  • The applicant must be in a good stage, physically and mentally both.
  • The candidate must have bachelor’s degree or must be completed a master’s degree
  • The maximum age of eligibility is 45 years.

Types of Assistance in Value of Great Wall Program

  • Tuition fees
  • Medical insurance premium
  • Stipend
  • Accommodation
View More ▼
6. Great Scholarships India
Great Scholarships India supports and encourages students who want to pursue higher studies in the UK. This scholarship allows one to complete postgraduate study in any participating UK universities. This scholarship is offered widely by 20 UK universities.

Eligibility Criteria of Great Scholarships India

  • The applicant should be a great scholarship alumnus. The candidate should be a citizen and present resident of India, China, Indonesia, Turkey, Malaysia, or Thailand.
  • Must have completed a UG degree in his/her hometown.
  • Should be applying to one of the participating UK universities.

Types of Assistance in Value of Great Scholarships India

  • Maximum funding up to USD 10,000.
  • Tuition fees
  • One-way travel.
  • Health allowance
  • Adequate living expenses
View More ▼
7. Fulbright-Kalam Climate Fellowship
UNICEF sponsors Fulbright-Kalam Climate Fellowship to address climate change-related issues by encouraging scientific and technical researchers scholars from India and the USA. The fellowships are offered to doctoral, academic, and professional research. 20 UK universities.

Eligibility Criteria of Fulbright-Kalam Climate Fellowship

  • Applicants must be registered for their Ph.D. and have conducted adequate research in the relevant field.
  • Employed applicants must follow the instructions regarding the employer’s endorsement.

Types of Assistance in Value of Fulbright-Kalam Climate Fellowship

  • J – 1 visa support
  • A monthly stipend
  • Coverage of accidents and sickness as per US government guidelines.
  • Setting-in and professionally allowance
  • Great opportunity for non-audit courses and gaining work experience.
View More ▼
8. The Inlaks Shivdasani Scholarships
The Inlaks Shivdasani scholarships are very helpful to the students who want to take education from well-recognized universities in the US. Since 1976, these scholarships have helped Indian students to take higher educations. These scholarships award up to USD 100,000 to cover scholar’s expenses and tuition fees.

Eligibility Criteria of The Inlaks Shivdasani Scholarships

  • These scholarships are open to Indian students who have been residents in India from the past 6 months.
  • For mathematics, science environment, and their related subjects, minimum academic grade of 70% from a recognized university.
  • For Social science, humanities, law, fine arts, architecture, and their related subjects, a minimum academic grade of 65% from a recognized university.

Types of Assistance in Value of The Inlaks Shivdasani Scholarships

  • It covers the total tuition fees
  • Health allowance
  • One-way Travel allowance
  • Adequate living expenses
